Kiddo brought this back from a Ren Faire for me, and it is great. You can taste cherry (a lot of it), and there are clear hints of vanilla and an aftertaste of peppercorn in the chai. It's better sipped over the course of a morning than finished in a rush, but I'm looking forward to going through this whole bag.
